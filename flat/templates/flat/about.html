About Page

Roomies is a web app made with Django framework.
It let you manage your household chores and time!
- Register and create your new flat, then other users can enter it within name and password!
- Update your user data and profile photo!
- Notify your roommates about parties, events, whatever you want with 'Announcements'!
- Make shopping lists!
- Add periodic chores like hoovering and let our app split up them for you!
- Use it as a reminder of what you should do! {% endblock content %}
